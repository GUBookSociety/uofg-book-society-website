About Us

The Glasgow University Book Society is a new society that welcomes all that love reading and discussing books. We take suggestions for books on a monthly basis and then hold a vote to decide on the book we will read in the coming month.

At the end of said month we will hold an informal and relaxed discussion on the theme, character, prose or anything that comes to mind.

As our member pool grows we imagine we may hold more than one monthly book at a time and that members may find others with similar reading interests to organise their own discussions together.

Events

Being a new society we are very open to suggestions however asides reading our social activities may include going to the pub, watching movie adaptations of books and book swaps.

We have plenty of social members as is so please do not feel pressured to read any books you do not want to.

Contact Us

If you have any suggestions please get in touch either on our Facebook where we are regularly active, or through our email address: gubooksoc@mail.com.

To suggest a book for the monthly vote check our Facebook page for the most recent announcement that voting is open.

FAQ

How do I join?
Follow our facebook page, any events listed are open to anyone to attend. Membership is completely FREE. Do I need to buy the monthly book? Only if you want to read it. We are hoping to strike some sort of deal with a local bookseller in the future to make this cheaper for everyone.
